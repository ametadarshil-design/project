WELCOME TO RAJASTHAN!

Rajasthan, known as the "Land of Kings" and India's largest desert state, is a captivating destination in northern India that mesmerise travellers from around the world. The state presents a harmonious blend of lively cities, hospitable locals, exquisite cuisine, majestic palaces, and grand forts. Rajasthan offers a perfect travel experience, seamlessly fusing timeless allure with contemporary charm. This enchanting state beckons you with a symphony of vibrant cultures, a canvas painted with tales of valour, and landscapes that will steal your heart

Rich Folk Music and Dance

Rajasthan's folk music, with instruments like the Sarangi and Morchang, is as soul-stirring as its desert landscape. The Kalbelia dance, performed by women in black swirling skirts, is internationally renowned for its grace and rhythm.

Authentic Rajasthani Cuisine

Indulge in a traditional Rajasthani thali that includes dishes like Dal Baati Churma, Gatte ki Sabzi, Ker Sangri, and Bajre ki Roti. The food is rich in flavor, often spicy, and prepared with love using native ingredients.

Rajasthan Festival

Rajasthan, India, is renowned for its vibrant and diverse festivals, showcasing the state's rich cultural heritage and traditions. These festivals, both religious and historical, attract tourists and locals alike, contributing to the preservation of culture and heritage. Some popular festivals include the Teej Festival, Pushkar Fair, Bikaner Camel Festival, and the Desert Festival in Jaisalmer.
